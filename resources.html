Common Questions

Everything you need to know about starting your workspace wellness journey.

How often should I move?

The 20-20-20 rule is a great start (every 20 mins, look 20 feet away for 20 seconds). For physical movement, aim to stand or stretch for at least 2 minutes every hour.

Can I do these in a communal office?

Absolutely! The exercises on the "Sitting" page are subtle and perfect for open-plan offices. Encouraging your colleagues to join in creates a more supportive culture.

Do I need special equipment?

No. All exercises are designed to be done with just your body weight and standard office furniture like a chair or desk.

What if I have a pre-existing injury?

Always listen to your body. If an exercise causes sharp pain, stop immediately and consult a professional. The exercises here are generally light and designed for preventative maintenance.

Is movement actually productive?

Yes. Movement breaks increase oxygen flow to the brain, which enhances problem-solving abilities and prevents the "brain fog" that often occurs in the afternoon.
